Similar/Fall-Themed Drinks

Aside from the iconic Pumpkin Spice Lattes, various coffee chains sell other kinds of drinks in the fall. Some still consist of pumpkin, while others were more original or had more unique changes. Some aren’t even coffee at all. Some examples of fall themed drinks from coffee chains include:

  • Starbucks sells other pumpkin drinks in the fall, like Pumpkin Cream Cold Brew and Iced Pumpkin Cream Chai. They also sell the iced version of their Pumpkin Spice Latte, for anyone who prefers it cold.
  • Dunkin Donuts is selling Sabrina’s Daydream Refreshers, which are cold foam drinks made from fruits, like strawberry and mango. They also have Candy Bar Iced Signature Lattes, which are regular lattes with various kinds of candy topped on it.
  • Peet’s coffee sells Pumpkin Lattes, which taste like pumpkin pie, and Pumpkin Chai Lattes, which are closer to Pumpkin Spice Lattes. They also have Vanilla Iced Lattes With Coffee Popping Pearls, similar to a boba tea drink. They also have halloween-themed drinks like the “Zombie Brew”, which is cold brew made with vanilla syrup and contains green-dyed matcha-vanilla cold foam at the top.
  • Dutch bros is selling its Pumpkin Spice Lattes, alongside their Caramel Pumpkin Brulees and Cookie Butter Lattes. They’re also selling something new: the Candied Cherry Rebel, which is not a coffee, but a cherry flavored energy drink with a cherry float. A similar drink they’re selling is the Trick-Or-Treat rebel, which is a strawberry, almond, and white chocolate energy drink, with trick-or-treat gummies on top.
