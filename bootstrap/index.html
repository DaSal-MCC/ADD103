What is your favorite holiday?

Halloween takes place in October. It's about dressing up in costumes and knocking on doors to get candy. This activity is mostly done by children, but adults can also celebrate the holiday by throwing parties.

Thanksgiving takes place in November. It's about giving thanks to friends and family while enjoying a wonderful dinner consisting of Turkey and other goods. It's important to celebrate those who help create America.

Christmas takes place in December. It's about celebrating the birth of Christ and opening up presents under a festive tree. Christmas is the season of giving and loving.

It's okay if you like multiple of these holidays. In fact, it's great if you like ALL of the holidays!

It's important to celebrate these holidays because of their historical significance. People around the world celebrate all of these holidays!
