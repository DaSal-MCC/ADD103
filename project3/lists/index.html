How to carve a pumpkin

  1. Cut a hole on top of a pumpkin and empty out the pumpkin innards.
  2. Draw the face you want to carve on a piece of paper.
  3. Draw that same face on the pumpkin with a Sharpie.
  4. Cut out the shapes from the face of the pumpkin.
  5. Place inside the pumpkin a lit candle, place it outside, and enjoy!

Glossary

Apple
A round fruit grows on trees and can be cut into slices. Can be colored red, yellow, or green.
Pumpkin
A round vegetable that grows from a vine; is usually orange and can be used for cooking or jack-o-lanterns.
Latte
A type of drink that contains coffee, steamed milk, creamer, and other add-ons like spices.
