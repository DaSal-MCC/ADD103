What are grass Pokemon?

Grass Pokemon are a basic and well-known Pokemon type that are mixed between collection and play. Some well-known and powerful cards consist of Pokemon like Venusaur and Celebi. Some other powerful Pokemon include:

  • Leafeon (ex, EX, GX, V, VMax, VStar)
  • Virizion (GX)
  • Iron Leaves (ex)

Some fun facts:

  • There is a special version of a card, Volcarona, who is a dual-type Pokemon that is both grass and fire. The irony in this is that grass types are weak to fire, and this Pokemon is both a fire type and weak to fire.
  • Some other cards include Venusaur and Celebi tag-team GX; a special card that consists of two Pokemon. It is quite powerful, primarisly for it's attack, Pollen Hazard, that inflicts 50 damage, but makes the opponent's Pokemon burned (which makes them take damage overtime), Poisoned (ditto), and confused (has a chance to make your opponent's Pokemon's attack miss.), all at once.
