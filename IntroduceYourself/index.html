About Me

Hi! I'm David, and this is my website. Here you'll get to view mostly everything about me, primairly some background information and my interests in Pokemon cards and Roblox studio coding. You can find the latter two in the links above, but this page contains all my background information. The image to the right is me. I'm attending MCC and aim to get a degree in software design and development, and my dream job is to be a video game developer. I've always loved video games growing up, and that has inspired me to make my own, where I can not only make money, but have fun doing it, too.

The image to the left is my workspace. When I do my college work or just relax, I do it here on my laptop, at my personal table. I have other items here, like candy, coffee, my headphones, and other things to display. I do my homework here, both for online and in-person classes, but when I'm not doing anything important, I like to play games on my laptop instead, or do other things like build lego sets, unbox Pokemon cards, or just sit down for a little while. I like to keep all my Rubik's Cubes and Pokemon cards here, because they're easy to access right away and they're on display for everyone to see.
