Coffees at the End of the Year

A list of Starbucks Coffees during Fall/Winter

This is a list of some iconic seasonal drinks that Starbucks sells (As of 2025):

  • Pumpkin Spice Latte
  • Pumpkin Cream Chai
  • Peppermint Mocha
  • White Chocolate Peppermint Mocha
  • Caramel Brulee Latte

Most will notice that seasonal drinks in coffee stores are usually at the end of the year, primarily around Halloween and Christmas. Starbucks sells their Pumpkin Spice Lattes around September (the beginning of Fall), and their Peppermint Mochas around December. A lot of other cafes and restaurants sell seasonal drinks. It's not just in-cafe though; lots of companies expand the festivities by advertising additional products, like creamers, to stores.
