Welcome to the (unoffical) Pokemon TCG website!

Here, you get to read all about the Pokemon Trading Card Game (TCG). You can read all about Pokemon cards and information about specific kinds of cards, from gameplay enhancing cards to rare cards. Scroll through the links at the top to begin!

Today, collecters and TCG players across the world are rapidly collecting Pokemon cards, either to create a large collection of various artworks and rare variants, or to gather the strongest and most powerful cards to win TCG battles. There are offical tournaments around the Trading Card Game, hosted by card stores or even the Pokemon Company themselves. There are prizes consisting of store credit and Pokemon packs, including an offical "Prize pack" by the company for these tournaments.

Pokemon cards existed all the way back 1996. They were created as a collabaration between the Pokemon Company and Game Freak, and were a part of a card game. The cards and their designs sparked in popularity, and became the most iconic of all card games. They have continued to rise in popularity since then, with more Pokemon being featured and the add-ons of rare variants, usually made of foil and with textured designs.
