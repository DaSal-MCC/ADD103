What kinds of sodas are there?

Many soda brands exist, and they all taste different. They also come with multiple flavors of their brand soda, the most common being cherry. Soda companies expand on their famous sodas to draw consumers and bring popularity amongst themselves.

There are several thousand soda brands out there, but the most well-known consist of the following:

  • Dr. Pepper
  • Coca-Cola
  • Fanta
  • Pepsi
  • Mountain Dew
  • Sprite
  • 7-Up
  • Sunkist

The basic soda flavors have their own taste. However, most brands have experimented with multiple flavor mixtures in their sodas. These commonly consist of artifical fruit flavor.

  • Cherry (The Most Common)
  • Orange
  • Blueberry
  • Cream/Vanilla
  • Strawberry
  • Coffee
  • Grape
