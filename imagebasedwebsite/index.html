Welcome!

Hi! Welcome to my Pumpkin Spice Latte image website. Here, you’ll find all things Pumpkin Spice Lattes, along with other seasonal drinks, like Pumpkin Cream Cold Brew. This website primarily focuses on Pumpkin Spice Lattes though, and you can explore all kinds of information about them, like how you can make them, where they originated from, drinks similar to them or themed around fall, and which cafes you can find these lattes at. There are a bunch of images included to help give an idea of what they look like, which you’ll see on sections like the How To Make page. The images on the right show various Pumpkin Spice Lattes you can make or find, and you’ll see even more later on. My website is themed around Pumpkin Spice Lattes because right now, it's the season where they’re out in cafes. They’re also in stores in the form of coffee creamers and refrigerated drinks. I love Pumpkin Spice Lattes, and I enjoy drinking them whenever I can during the fall, which is the only time of the year where they’re found. I also enjoy eating Pumpkin Spice themed treats, like cake rolls or cupcakes, more of which you can read about in the other page. I hope that, by exploring this website, you’ll learn some new things about Pumpkin Spice Lattes, and that you’ll end up loving them too! The links at the top will guide you to various parts of this website, each containing different information. Happy searching and have fun!

David Salazar. 2025 McHenry County College.
