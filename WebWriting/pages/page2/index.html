What are water Pokemon?

Water Pokemon are a basic Pokemon type that a lot of people use and collect. They are popular in terms of playing the game, and for collecting. Some well-known and powerful cards consist of Gyrados and Palkia. Some other cards include:

  • Palafin ex
  • Blastoise ex
  • Greninja ex

Some fun facts:

  • Some water cards are themed around ice. Although ice-types exist in standard Pokemon, they are water Pokemon instead in the TCG series to keep things simple. Some ice-type water Pokemon include Glaceon (regular, V, VMax, VStar) and Abomasnow (regular and Mega ex). These Pokemon have steel as their weakness, instead of the standard electric or grass. They still do water-type damage, as do all water Pokemon.
  • Water Pokemon have a special attachable card that, if your Pokemon is knocked out in battle, will revive it by letting you put it back into your hand where you keep cards that you use.
