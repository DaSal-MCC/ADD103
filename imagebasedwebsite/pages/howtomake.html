How to make PSLS:

This recipe is from allRecipes by Zippy.

Click here to view the original recipe.

Equipment:

  • Blender
    		•
  • Coffee maker (no espresso machine needed)
    		•

    Prep time: 10 mins

    Cook time: 5 mins

    Total time: 15 mins

    Ingredients:

  • 3 cups hot whole milk
    		•
  • 4 teaspoons white sugar
    		•
  • ½ teaspoon vanilla extract
    		•
  • ½ teaspoon pumpkin pie spice
    		•
  • 6 ounces double-strength brewed coffee
    		•
  • 3 teaspoons sweetened whipped cream
    		•
  • 3 pinches pumpkin pie spice
    		•

    Step 1: Combine hot milk, sugar, vanilla extract, and pumpkin pie spice in a blender; blend until frothy.

    Step 2: Pour milk mixture into 3 coffee mugs to about ⅔ full; add 2 ounces coffee to each.

    Step 3: Garnish each mug with whipped topping and pumpkin pie spice.

    Servings Per Recipe: 3 cups
