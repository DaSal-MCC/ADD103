What are fire Pokemon?

Fire Pokemon are one of the most well-known kind of Pokemon card type you can obtain. There are common Fire Pokemon cards like the basic energy cards, the Ninetales evolution line and the Infernape evolution line, but then there are rarer and more powerful cards like Charizard (ex, GX, VMax, and VStar). Even more powerful cards include:

  • Reshiram EX
  • Gouging Fire ex
  • Arcanine ex

Some fun facts:

  • There are a lot of fire-type cards that exist, but only some are used in the TCG game for their abilities, like Entei ex.
  • Most of the powerful cards usually consist of Charizard, in terms of his ex, GX, VMax and VStar versions (especially his VMax evolution, which has an attack that deals 300 damage). In terms of market value, he's also one of the most valuable cards. A 1st edtion holo rare Charizard card in mint condition can go for about $100K!
