Where can you find PSLs?

There are a few cafes that sell Pumpkin Spice Lattes, along with various fall-themed treats:

  • Starbucks is well-known for their version of the Pumpkin Spice Latte, along with variants of it like the iced version and the frappuchino version. They also sell other seasonal drinks like the Pumpkin Cream Cold Brew, which you can read about in the Other Seasonal drinks section.
  • Dunkin Donuts sells Pumpkin Spice Lattes. They generally release theirs before Starbucks does, and their version consists of several ingredients, including caramel.
  • McDonalds’ McCafe sells Pumpkin Spice Lattes, containing the standard ingredients like pumpkin and cinnamon flavor, along with steamed whole or nonfat milk. They also sell seasonal treats like the Baked Apple Pie and the Pumpkin and Creme Pie.
  • Dutch Bros sells Pumpkin Spice Lattes, made with a unique Pumpkin purlee. They sell lattes, along with other seasonal drinks, like the Caramel Pumpkin Brulee, one of their iconic drinks.
  • Peets sells what they call a Pumpkin Latte, which tastes similar to pumpkin pie.

If you can’t find any of these cafes, you can also find coffee creamers in stores:

  • Starbucks sells Pumpkin Spice Latte coffee creamers in stores, which is based closely on their Pumpkin Spice Latte in their cafe. You can also find a non-dairy version of the creamer.
  • Chobani sells Pumpkin Spice creamer, along with an Oatmilk version of it.
  • Coffee mate sells Pumpkin Spice Creamer, which tastes like Pumpkin Pie, according to them.

If you prefer something closer to the real thing and not in creamer form, try these:

  • Starbucks sells an iced-espresso version of the Pumpkin Spice Latte in stores.
  • Dunkin Donuts sells iced Pumpkin Spice Latte drinks in stores.
